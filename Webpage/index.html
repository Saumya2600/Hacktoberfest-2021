Fate / Zero

Understanding the fate universe

In Fate universe long ago mages of powerful bloodlines created a battle royal known as The Holy Grail War. The Holy Grail is an omnipotent existence that can grant any wish and is the subject of the war. Each of The Holy Grail Wars usually revolves around seven Masters chosen by the Holy Grail and the seven Servants that they summon. Each Servant is a Heroic Spirit based on a real historical or legendary figure, such as King Arthur, Hercules, or William Shakespeare, but are usually referred to using the name of their class.
